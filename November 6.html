Emperor Hui Zong

I had hoped to have you read a biography of emperor huizong before coming to calss today from the history of the song, but I wasn't able to track down a good translation of it, but I'd like to talk about him anyway so we can discuss some of the historical background in which this novel is set

Emperor Huizong is perhaps most widely known as the emperor who allowed the jurchens to take over northern china, and for this reason he has been vilified in a lot of later periods. modern scholars have been working against this attitude (patricia ebrey in particular has been trying focus on the things he tried to do)

He was born in 1082 and died in 1135. He was the emperror between 1100 and 1126, when he was forced to abdicate the throne.

He was known as an excellent calligrapher and patron of the arts. He was famous for the way he promoted poetry, daoism, and usc

He was the 11th son of the Shenzong emperor and thus didn ot really expect to become emperor, but he did when emperor zhezhong died, because zhezong's heir had also already died.

The Water margin becomes a commentary on the issues of the time (and through that, a commentary on the issues of the yuan and the ming dynsty). Because huizong was generally regarded as incompent, contemporary people viewed him as allowing the bandits around the country to flourish.
