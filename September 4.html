Bi-syllabic Last Names

There are very few bi-syllabic last names in modern China: Ouyang, Sima, Situ, and Xiahou though only Ouyang is relatively common. IN the olden days tere were many more of these bi syllabic names: Ximen, Gongsun, Zhuge, etc.

Chapters 20 to 40, generally speaking

At this point in then novel, Cao Cao controls the emperor and thus the imperial court. He is also commanding some of his rivals, while others are not near enough to cause any trouble

Yet Cao Cao uses an imperial bow at a hunt, which upsets the actual emperor who asks Dong Cheng to take care of Cao Cao.

Liu Bei is tasked with offing Cao Cao and gets close but doesn't do it. Yuan Shao kills Gongsun Zan, who had mentored Liu Bei in other parts of the novel, so Liu wants to take care of him. Liu kills Yuan Shu (Yuan Shao's brother) but Cao Cao no longer has Liu nearby and sends someone to kill him, but Guan Yu stops it

At this point, Liu decides to join up with Yuan Shao to avoid being killed by Cao Cao

In Chapter 24, Cao Cao kills Dong Cheng and his family and starts actively guarding the Emperor. Guan Yu gets seperated from Liu Bei and Zhang Fei at this point, and Liu Bei loses a battle against Cao Cao (Yuan Shao who should have helped didn't as his son was sick).

Chapter 25 we will discuss in detail, but Guan Yu is captured by Cao Cao

Guan Yu fights for Cao Cao until he hears where Liu Bei is and then he bails. Cao Cao respects Guan's honor so lets him go, but doesn't provide him with any documentaiton, so Guan Yu runs into all sorts of trouble.

Liu Bei, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei are reunited, but Zhang Fei tthinks guan yu is a traitor because he was working for CC. GY kills some CC officers to show he never betryaed them. Liu Bei bails on Yuan Shao and they are finally back together. Zhao Zilong joins them!

At this point, Sun Ce (one of the eventual founders of Wu on e of the three kingdoms) jumps back in the action and plans to fight Cao Cao and Yuan Shao. Sun Ce is attacked and wounded before anything can happen. In the meantime he kilsl a sorcerer and is haunted by his ghosts, which eventually kills him but leaves everything to Sun Quan his brother

Yuan Shao attacks CC, but because he is a poor commander he eventually loses. Yuan tries to grogrpu and Cao Cao pursues. Liu Bei decides to try to take over the capitol but fails when CC comes back. Liu Biao then lets Liu Bei hang out with ihm. CC goes after Yuan Shao again, who dies soon after. His heirs fight after he appoints his younger rather than his older son as heir

CC finishes them off

Liu Bei is with Liu Biao, and LBiao's inlaws stir up trouble (which we will see in detail)

Liu Bei escapes a murder attempt and begins to recruit good advisors. Liu Biao is upset at his family and LB forgives him, helps defeat a CC atatck

Liu Bei adopts a son, which Guan Yu is pretty upset about

Liu tries to find Zhuge Liang, who eventually advises Liu to form a kingdom (the third to go alogn with Cao's Wei and Sun's Wu.

Some of Liu Biao's allies are killed and Biao wants to send LB out to the east, but Liu Bei is afraid of upseting the Suns so does not. Instead he goes to the north to defend against CC, where Zhuge's amazing military strategy defeats a much larger army.
