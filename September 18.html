After Liu Bei dies, Shu has to deal with some rebel uprisings in addition to the threates from Wu and Wei. Zhuge Liang sets up an alliance with Wu and repels an invasion by Cao Pi

The fight against the Man tribe, this is an interesting sub story in which they fight in the southern Jungles in what would now be southern China in Yunnan and in the jungles of northern burma

Zhuge captures and releases the rebel leader multiple times and beings to win over the Man people and eventual subdues them.

Cao Pi dies and Cao Rui takes over, in the meantime Zhuge Liang engineers a rift between Sima Yi, one of Cao's best advisors and Cao. When this happens, it is time to invade. They come close to defeating Wei but doesn't quite do it.

Jiang Wei, a Wei general prevents the capture of a third city and stalls the Shu attack. Jiang defeats Zhao Zilong, but defects to Shu after meeting Zhuge Liang.

Another rebellion happens and Zhuge's army is attacked from behind. They fend it off

Sima yi exposes someone who is going to defect saving Wei. Things turn against the Shu a bit here, when Zhuge Liang trusts generals who end up failing

Zhao Zilong dies of natural causes, and this leads to the gradual decline of the Shu armies.

Zhuge has back and forth victories and is about to press his advantage when Wei spies make the emperor think Zhuge is goign to depose him, so he can't take advantage.

Zhuge fvandes multiple times but is getting old. He starts a ritual that will allow him to continue to live for 12 more years, but is interupted. Jiang Wei receives Zhuge's teachings and Zhuge passes away.

Cao Rui, no longer harried by Zhuge, becomes a despot

Sima Yi puts down rebellions, but is mistreated by the Cao family. Eventually he plots a coup and takes over (though he does not depose the young Cao Fang on the throne.

Jiang Wei invades but is defeated

Sima Yi dies of old age as does Sun Quan. While Wu is mourning Sun Quan, Sima Shi (sima yi's son) invades). They fail and Wu invades back but fails miserablly.

Jiang Wei tries to invade again and fails, Cao Fang tries to plot against Sima Shi but the plot is uncovered, and Cao Fang is replaced by Cao Mao.

Sima Shi dies of cancer, and his brother Sima Zhao takes over. Sima Shi's general Deng Ai hands a major defeat to Wu

Wu meanwhile is realing from internal strife in which members of the Sun clan depose and kill each other.

Jiang Wei continues to fight Wei but rumors swirl and he is recalled

Euncuchs rise in Shu and wreak havoc (note the circularity of the novel).

Taking advantage of the Eunch Huang Hao, Sima Zhao invades as the eunuch keeps information away from the empeoror.

The Shu emperor surrenders ending the Liu line. Jiang Wei meanwhile refuses to surrender and begins to plot in private and Deng Ai is arrested.
